Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can I play the game offline?
    Yes, you can enjoy all puzzles without an internet connection. It’s perfect for travel, breaks, or quiet time anywhere. You don’t need Wi-Fi to relax and play. Everything is available offline once installed.
  • Are there different difficulty levels?
    Yes, you can choose how many pieces you want in each puzzle. So you can make it easier when you're tired or harder when you're feeling up for a challenge. Beginners can start easy, while experienced players can challenge themselves. You’re always in control of the experience.
  • What kind of images are included?
    The game features a wide variety of beautiful beach scenes. You’ll find tropical islands, ocean views, sunsets, and peaceful coastlines. The photos look amazing and they're really crisp - perfect when you just want to zone out. We keep adding new beaches so you won't get bored.
  • Can I use completed puzzles as wallpapers?
    Yes, once you complete a puzzle, you can save it as a wallpaper. So you can keep the ones you really love on your phone. It’s a nice way to personalize your device. It's pretty cool that you get to keep something after you're done playing.
